Quality Certified by the Government -The name ‘Ceylon Tea’ and the famous Lion logo that goes with it indicates the tea grown, manufactured and packed entirely in Sri Lanka conforming to strict quality standards laid down and administered by the Sri Lanka Tea Board.

VALUE ADDED TEA

We export more than 50% of tea in value added form. The Value added product range of Sri Lankan tea includes green tea, flavoured tea, organic tea, instant tea, iced tea, and ready-to-drink tea.Tea based soap, bath gel, shampoo and cosmetic products have recently been added to this product range.

Capable of winning over even the most discerning tea tasters, Sri Lankan tea comes in an array of flavours that has served the world for centuries. QUALITY STANDARDS

The Sri Lankan Tea industry maintains the highest quality in the world market and ISO 3720 is the minimum standard applied for the products. The Country has the capability to produce the cleanest tea in the world in terms of minimum pesticides and residues.

Methyl Bromide was removed from the production process in 2012. Therefore, Sri Lankan tea has been identified as an “ozone free tea” in the world trade. Sri Lanka is also adjusting well to the stringent ISO 22000 series and to the health & safety regulations stipulated by the European Community.
